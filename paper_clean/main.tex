\begin{abstract}
This report details the implementation and comparative evaluation of state-of-the-art time-series imputation models on the MIMIC-IV clinical dataset. We present a comprehensive benchmarking of deep learning baselines, knowledge-guided imputation (KGI) methods, and our proposed multimodal dynamic textual KGI approach. The evaluation spans both point-wise imputation accuracy and performance on a downstream clinical task (mortality prediction), highlighting the relative advantages of data-driven vs. knowledge-driven methodologies in modern healthcare AI.
\end{abstract}
